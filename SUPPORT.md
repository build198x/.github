# Support

Questions and ideas are welcome — especially now, while the shape of Build198x is
still open.

- **Ideas or "should it work like this?"** — open an issue or a discussion in the
  [build198x](https://github.com/build198x) org.
- **Status** — Build198x is deferred behind Asm198x and Emu198x maturing; see the
  org profile page.
- **Security** — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md); please report privately.

It's a small, early, not-yet-built project, so replies may take a little while.
Thanks for your patience.
